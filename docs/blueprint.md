# **App Name**: OptiServe ERP

## Core Features:

- Unified Dashboard: Provides a central control panel with real-time occupancy for hotels/lodges, table availability for restaurants, quick stats on guests, bookings, maintenance tasks, and cross-department analytics.
- Guest & Booking Management: Manages guest bookings, KYC, and preferences through API integrations (OYO, Trivago, MakeMyTrip, Swiggy, Zomato), direct booking module, KYC scanner with OCR and face match, and guest profile builder. Assigns rooms and tracks guests throughout their stay.
- Housekeeping & Maintenance Tracking: Tracks cleanliness and maintenance status by room. It also has the ability to auto assign staff tasks. Staff updates will be visible by QR check in/out logs
- Restaurant & Room Service Management: Connects waiters, kitchen, and billing with digital table layouts and waiter tablets. Tracks order statuses and integrates with Zomato, Swiggy, and UberEats to provide seamless room service order and menu management.
- Inventory & Stock Management: Efficiently manages perishable and non-perishable items with categorization, auto stock deduction, low stock alerts, vendor management, and purchase order tracking. AI-based prediction recommends optimal inventory levels and alerts to guide timely actions from the user: A tool that decides, based on past stock performance, when it will run out of stock and make the purchase suggestions
- Staff & Role Management: Manages employees and tracks work through role-based dashboard access, biometric/mobile attendance, work reports, task tracking, and internal chat. Staff fingerprint login for extra security
- Billing & Accounting: Provides a unified billing system with multi-mode payment options, automatic merging of restaurant bills to room bills, tax/invoice generation, split bill functionality, and analytics on spending patterns.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Navy (#0B132B) for a professional and premium feel.
- Accent color: Crimson Red (#B22222) for highlights and calls to action.
- Background color: Desaturated navy (approximately #161E36).
- Headings font: 'Poppins' sans-serif font, bold.
- Body font: 'Inter' sans-serif font, regular.
- Lucide-react for premium icons
- Blend of Glassmorphism and Neumorphism, 3D floating cards with subtle shadows to create depth, consistent visual hierarchy, and spacing.
- Framer Motion for gradual motion transitions, hover micro-interactions to engage users.
